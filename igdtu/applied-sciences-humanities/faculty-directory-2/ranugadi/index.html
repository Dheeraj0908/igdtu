Department of Applied Science and Humanities Faculty Profile

Name

Prof. (Dr.) Ranu Gadi

Qualifications 

Ph.D. (Chemistry) IIT Roorkee, Post Doctoral Fellowship (Environmental Sciences), Lancaster University, Lancaster, U.K.

Designation 

Professor (Chemistry), Dean (Industrial Res. & Development and Res. & Consultancy), Director (Anveshan Foundation)

Teaching Interests 

Applied Chemistry, Environmental Sciences, Renewable Energy Sources &Technologies, Environmental Impact Assessment, Environmental Management System

Office Address 

Room No. 108, IT and App. Science Block

Office Phone Number 

01123900234

Email 

ranugadi@igdtuw.ac.in

Research Areas 

Atmospheric Chemistry and Climate Change, Biomass emissions, Characterization of atmospheric aerosols (elemental, organics, carbonaceous, metals), Environmental Analytical Chemistry, Speciation and Bioremediation of priority pollutants in water/wastewater.
